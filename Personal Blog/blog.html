Before writing about anything you need to have a deep knowledge on that particular topic because it will not only help you in writing content but also help others to understand what you are trying to share with them.


Starting a blog is not about buying a domain

Proper research is very important part you need to take care of before starting a new blog because without research a blog is like a rotten tomato which is useless.

What Are LSI Keywords?

Latent Semantic Indexing are keywords related to your main keyword, these keywords are words that are frequently found together because they share the same context. For examples your page or blog is about ‘Mobile Phone’ then you could be writing about any of the following context related to ‘Mobile Phone’
  • Best Mobile Phone
  • Mobile Phone Comparison
  • Mobile Phone By Price
